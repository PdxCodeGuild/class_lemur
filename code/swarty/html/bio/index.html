Shuji Nakamura

Made Brilliance a career

Shuji Nakamura improved lights, lasers, and diode manufacturing by leaps and bounds

Basically, I like research because research is like to solve the quiz, you know. Always there is a problem, and I have to solve the problem. So I like those patterns. It's almost like research is sort of in a quiz.

Shuji Nakamura

Inventions:

  • Created inexpensive manufacturing process to creata GaN Leds, long thought to be uneconomical to produce
  • Blue Led he created was 3x brighter than any made before
  • Followed that by creating blue laser diodes used in Blu-ray and HD-DVD
  • He also invented the manufacturing process for blue and white LEDs in use today
  • Currently improving microLED manufacturing and technology
