Artoria Pendragon (a.k.a. Saber)

"There are no regrets. If one can be proud of one's life, one should not wish for another chance." -Saber

Artoria Pendragon is a female version of King Arthur. She is one of the main characters of Fate/Zero and one of the three main heroines of Fate/stay night. She is the Saber-class Servant of Kiritsugu Emiya in the Fourth Holy Grail War and Shirou Emiya in the Fifth Holy Grail War.
