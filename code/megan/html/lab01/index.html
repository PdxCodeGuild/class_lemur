Alexis Rose

“I don’t skate through life, David. I walk through life, in really nice shoes.”

Alexis Rose is the daughter of Johnny and Moira Rose. She has one older brother, David Rose, a former gallerist and current owner of Rose Apothecary. The Roses were once a very wealthy and influencial family, having made their money from Johnny's video rental store, Rose Video and Moira's job as the star of a wildy successsful soap opera, Sunrise Bay. The Roses ended up losing their fortune in a bad business deal and were forced to move to a town they owned, Schitt's Creek. Prior to her family losing their money, Alexis enjoyed spending her time flying around the world and reaping the benefits of her family's wealth. While in Schitt's Creek, she finally finished high school and went on to get a degree in public relations.

Alexis currently resides in New York City, where she is the CEO and Founder of Alexis Rose Communications, a PR firm.

A few places she's lived throughout her life:

  • Los Angeles
  • Schitt's Creek
  • New York
